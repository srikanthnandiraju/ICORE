ICORE PLATFORM 

Data and Information silos are unfortunately facts of life in life science and health care industry. Although not impossible but it is not easy to get an access to clinical data, lab data, medical images etc., which is needed by Researchers and Clinicians alike in their daily routine work.


Our goal is to address this very unaddressed problem, connect the disconnected data silos, break down the walls and artificial barriers, integrate all the information and provide a “single view of the patient (SVP)” so that researchers and clinicians can take the critical decisions more efficiently, improving the quality and speed of care and reducing the cost of care.
